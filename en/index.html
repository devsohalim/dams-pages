Overview

The farther you step from the crowds,
the deeper the rest feels

Dams is a private two-story pool villa in Namwon-eup, Seogwipo, on the quieter side of Jeju. Past the garden path lined with tangerine trees and traditional stone walls, you'll find a stay designed for calm mornings, unhurried afternoons, and private evenings with the people you came to be with.

The villa offers 3 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms, generous shared spaces, a private pool, and terraces that open toward the sea. It works especially well for families, multi-generational trips, and small groups of up to 8 or 9 guests looking for an entire private place in Jeju.

Entire villa, fully private
